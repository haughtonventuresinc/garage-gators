      OUR REPAIR SERVICES

      01.

      Spring & Cable Repair

      Our expert technicians are trained to quickly diagnose and repair or replace torsion and extension springs and lift cables, restoring your garage door system’s full functionality and safety.

      02.

      Keypad Repair

      We provide reliable garage door opener repair and reprogramming services, ensuring seamless access control and restoring convenience for your home or business.

      03.

      Motor Repair

      Garage Up specializes in diagnosing and repairing motor issues, whether a gear failure, electrical fault, or worn-out components.

      04.

      Sensor Repair

      If your door reverses unexpectedly or refuses to close, faulty sensors might be to blame. Our team will repair or replace your sensors to restore reliable, safe operation.

      05.

      Roller & Track Alignment Repair

      Noisy operation or a garage door that jerks or sticks may be due to misaligned tracks or worn rollers. We repair and realign tracks, replace damaged rollers, and ensure smooth, quiet door movement.

      06.

      Panel Repair

      Our technicians are trained to repair single-panel or multiple-panel garage doors. We offer precise repairs to match your door’s design and restore curb appeal.

      07.

      Commercial Repair

      We offer prompt, durable garage door repairs for all types of commercial garage doors to minimize downtime and ensure reliable performance.

      Common signs include the door not opening or closing properly, unusual noises during operation, sagging sections, slow response times, and visible damage to the door or its components.

      Regular maintenance is key to preventing major repairs. We recommend having your garage door inspected and serviced at least once a year. This includes checking and lubricating all moving parts, inspecting the door balance, and ensuring all safety features function correctly.

      If your garage door is over 15 years old, frequently needs repairs, or shows signs of significant wear and damage, it might be more cost-effective to replace it. Our experts can assess your door and recommend whether you need a garage door spring repair or a simple maintenance task like lubrication.

      If your garage door is stuck, do not attempt to force it open or closed. This could cause further damage or pose a safety risk. Instead, contact us immediately for emergency repair services. Our technicians are trained to safely resolve these issues.

      Repair costs vary depending on the type of repair needed. Simple fixes may cost as little as $100, while more extensive repairs, such as replacing springs or cables, can range from $400 to $800 or more. We provide detailed estimates before starting any repair work.

      While homeowners can perform minor maintenance tasks like lubrication, more complex repairs, such as fixing broken springs, should be handled by professionals to ensure safety and proper functioning.
