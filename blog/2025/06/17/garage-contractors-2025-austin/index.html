      Common Garage Problems That Call for a Pro

      Sometimes it’s obvious you need help—like when your garage door crashes to the ground. But other times, the signs are more subtle.

      Here are common issues that signal it’s time to call in a pro:

      • Broken Springs: These are under extreme tension and dangerous to fix without training.
      • Off-Track Doors: The door looks crooked or grinds when moving.
      • Stuck or Dead Openers: Your remote works, but the door won’t budge.
      • Loud or Shaky Doors: Squeaks and jerks often mean rollers or tracks are damaged.
      • Unreliable Sensors: Your door reverses for no reason or won’t close at all.
      • Heavy or Unbalanced Doors: This could mean cable or spring issues, especially if your door won’t stay up manually.

      If you’re experiencing any of these, don’t risk a DIY disaster. Garage Up Austin’s garage contractors handle these problems every day and knows how to fix them safely and quickly.
